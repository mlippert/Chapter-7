Stereoisomerism

Looking back:

To begin this chapter you should be familiar with: bonding, molecular geometry, structure, and conformations of molecules.

To be discovered:

In this chapter, you will explore the symmetry and asymmetry of molecules. The arrangement of atoms in space for a molecule has consequences for its identity, physical properties, and reactivity. Two compounds displaying identical atomic connectivity, but with a different three-dimensional atomic arrangement, may exhibit significantly dissimilar behavior.

Chapter Skills

1. Identifying symmetry elements
2. Identifying stereogenic centers
3. Assigning stereochemical configuration
4. Drawing stereoisomers
5. Identifying a meso compound
6. Calculating specific rotation

Looking forward:

Stereoisomerism permeates nearly all aspects of organic chemistry. It is an inseparable part of many chemical reactions. Recognition of when and how it is applied is a required skill. Every chapter that follows will have some degree of dependency on stereoisomerism.
